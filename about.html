Hello! I am a graduate student at GIST. My dream is the universalization of intelligence through the development of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI). To contribute to this goal, my main research interests are focused on value alignment, safety, and reasoning.

Currently, I am conducting research at the Data Science Laboratory under the supervision of Professor Sundong Kim. My work is primarily centered on Large Language Models (LLMs), and I am actively researching topics including reinforcement learning, the analysis of LLMs using Sparse Autoencoders, and developing data augmentation and learning frameworks.
